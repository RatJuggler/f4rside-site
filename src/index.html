Bio

Hey, I’m Damon! I am a music tutor who recently graduated from Plymouth University with a First-Class Honours in Music. I have a year of tutoring experience as well as Grade 8 and ARSM diploma in Piano Performance. I also make electronic music and digital art here.

Online Tutoring

  • Online

    Lessons are held online via Skype or Zoom, typically once per week. I use a combination of drawing software, notation software and demonstration on my own piano to teach.

  • Theory

    Music theory from beginner up to ABRSM Grade 8 or equivalent level. Lessons will be catered to your needs, whether that is to prepare for an exam, improve compositional skills, or for general musicianship. I use a mixture of lecture-style explanations of topics, practice questions and practical demonstrations.

  • Piano

    Piano from beginner up to ABRSM Grade 8 or equivalent level. Lessons will be catered to your needs, whether that is preparation for an exam or performance, improving skills for musical study, or simply for leisure. I use some technical exercises but focus mainly on the music which the student wishes to play.
